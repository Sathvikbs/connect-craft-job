Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Data collection

JobConnect stores all user data (profiles, jobs, applications) locally in your browser using LocalStorage. No data is transmitted to external servers.

2. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Only local preferences (like theme) are stored.

3. Third parties

We do not share data with any third parties.

4. Security

Because data lives only in your browser, clearing browser storage will erase your JobConnect data. Export your profile regularly to back it up.

5. Children

The service is not directed to users under 13.

6. Contact

Questions? Visit our Contact page.
