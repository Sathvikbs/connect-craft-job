Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about JobConnect.

Is JobConnect free to use?

Yes! JobConnect is completely free for both candidates and employers as part of this mini project.

How do I apply for a job?

Sign in as a candidate, open any job and click "Apply now". You can attach a resume, portfolio link, and cover letter.

Can employers post multiple jobs?

Absolutely — from the employer dashboard, click "Post new job" any number of times.

Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. Nothing leaves your device.

How do I reset the demo data?

Press Shift + Alt + R anywhere on the site to reseed all users, jobs and applications.

Can I export my profile?

Yes — from the Profile page, click "Export profile" to download a JSON backup you can re-import anytime.

Does the resume builder support printing?

Yes. Open the resume builder and click "Print" or "Download" to save an HTML file you can print to PDF.

Is there a dark mode?

Yes — click the 🌙 icon in the top nav. Your preference is remembered.
